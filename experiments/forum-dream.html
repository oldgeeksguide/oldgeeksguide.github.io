Intel® XDK

Getting the most from this forum

This is the place to ask questions of and share information with other users of the Intel® XDK. Use the Intel® XDK to develop HTML5 apps.

Check out our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) for some of the answers.

Please do not include private information such as your email address in your posts. If you need to share private information with an Intel employee, they can start a private thread with you.

The Intel XDK Support team is best able to support Monday through Friday: 8:00 – 17:00 (Pacific Time).

To get the best support possible, please include the following information with your post:

  • Intel XDK version (upper right corner of the Intel XDK).
  • Clear explanation of your issue.
  • How to reproduce your problem.
  • Platform you are building for, build log, build settings, etc.
  • Screenshots, when applicable.

Help Us Help You:

  • Attach build logs to your post instead of pasting them directly into the post.
  • If you are adding to a thread, please stick to the subject of the thread; otherwise, start a new one.
  • Post messages publicly whenever possible.
Intel monitors this forum Monday – Friday, 09:00 – 17:00 Pacific Time (GMT –7:00). Depending on the amount of research we need to do to track down the answer, it may take a day or two for us to respond.
